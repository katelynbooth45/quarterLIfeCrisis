Is the End Nearing for Cable Dominance?

February 15, 2019 by Katelyn Booth

Could this be the end of traditional cable entertainment? With cable prices skyrocketing, consumers are trending towards more affordable options and companies are taking steps to respond to changing demand.

In 2007, Netflix announced its streaming service idea after they gained over one million subscribers from their interpretation of the tradition pay-to-rent service model. Since then other companies have followed suit, such as Amazon's response by adding Amazon Prime Video to their membership revenue stream, and Disney recently announced its plans for Disney+ after ending the Marvel relationship with Netflix.
